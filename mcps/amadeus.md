## Description
Connect the **Amadeus** global travel platform to any AI agent — the backbone of the travel industry.

### What you can do
- **Flights** — Search, compare prices, airlines, durations, stops, and cabin classes
- **Hotels** — Find hotels by city or GPS coordinates with rates and availability
- **Transfers** — Airport pickup and point-to-point transfer quotes
- **Activities** — Tours, experiences, and things to do at any destination
- **Inspirations** — Discover cheapest destinations from any airport
- **Flight Status** — Real-time schedules, terminals, and gates

### Who is this for?
- **Travel Agencies** — Instant multi-destination quotes
- **Corporate Travel** — Policy-compliant booking search
- **Travelers** — "Where can I fly cheaply from Paris this weekend?"
