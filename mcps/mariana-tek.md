Manage classes, instructors, members, reservations, and pricing for your boutique fitness studio powered by Mariana Tek through natural conversation.

## Description
Connect your **Mariana Tek** studio to any AI agent and manage your premium boutique fitness business through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Classes** — View scheduled sessions, class types, capacity, and waitlists
- **Instructors** — List star instructors with bios, specialties, and schedules
- **Members** — Search customers, view credits, attendance streaks, and engagement
- **Reservations** — Track bookings, spot assignments, and check-in status
- **Memberships** — Browse unlimited plans, class packs, and corporate packages
- **Pricing** — View all pricing tiers and promotional offers
- **Locations** — Manage multiple studio locations and room configurations

### Who is this for?

- **SoulCycle, Barry's, Rumble** — Built for the world's top boutique fitness brands
- **Studio Owners** — Real-time class fill rates, revenue, and member insights
- **Operations Managers** — Multi-location scheduling and instructor coverage
- **Marketing Teams** — Member engagement data for retention campaigns
