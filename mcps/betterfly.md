## Description
Connect your **Betterfly** Corporate API to any AI agent and bring ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) analytics naturally into your chats.

### What you can do

- **Impact Analytics** — Aggregate your entire company's donations (arising from employees burning Bettercoins synced from physical exercise).
- **User Engagement** — Check an employee’s participation rate, specific metrics, or overall unspent Bettercoins inside the reward marketplace.
- **Challenges & Activities** — See the historical trail of burned calories, recorded steps, or internal corporate races occurring right now.
- **Insurtech & Benefits** — Dynamically list available sub-benefits included in your corporate policy.

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Introduce your highly secure Operator API Key
3. Enjoy precise corporate wellness insights injected onto Claude, Cursor, or your local machine terminal

### Who is this for?

- **HR Leadership & Chief Happiness Officers** — Track if your internal health policies are converting into real mental wellness.
- **ESG Managers** — Fetch certified macro-reports of charitable donations globally generated by your talent hub.
- **DevOps & Internal Comms** — Build dynamic Slack/Discord internal bots referencing these precise endpoints via natural language prompts.


## Available Tools
- **assign_reward**: Allocate manual recognition bonus
- **create_challenge**: Initialize team activity challenge
- **get_company_metrics**: Get aggregate well-being stats
- **get_impact_stats**: Measure ESG and social metrics
- **get_user_rewards**: Get user unlocked rewards
- **get_user**: Get user wellness details
- **list_activities**: It can track global corporate steps or an individual’s synced activities.

List wellness activities
- **list_benefits**: List accessible benefits
- **list_challenges**: List active corporate challenges
- **list_donations**: List corporate donations
- **list_insurance**: Fetch covered scopes
- **list_partners**: Evaluate linked ecosystem partners
- **list_users**: List corporate users (members)
- **pause_user**: Temporarily restrict a user account
