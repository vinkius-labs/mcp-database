Manage video rooms, active sessions, and participants on 100ms — the powerful live video infrastructure.

## Description
Connect your **100ms** account to any AI agent and manage your live video infrastructure through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Room Management** — List all virtual rooms, retrieve deep technical metadata, and create new rooms with specific templates and descriptions
- **Session Monitoring** — Monitor active or completed video sessions in real-time and retrieve session history across your organization
- **Participant Governance** — List all peers (participants) currently in a session and retrieve their unique IDs and roles
- **Peer Control** — Remotely remove or kick participants from active sessions with custom reasons directly from your agent
- **Recording Discovery** — List and browse cloud recordings, filtered by room or status (completed, failed, or processing)
- **Operational Insights** — Quickly find unique room, session, and peer IDs required for automated video workflows
- **Scalable Infrastructure** — Verify your live video configurations and template settings through automated metadata retrieval

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your 100ms Management Token
3. Start managing your video rooms and participants through Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client

No more manual navigation through complex video dashboards to kick a participant. Your AI agent becomes your video operations assistant.

### Who is this for?

- **Platform Engineers** — monitor active video sessions and troubleshoot participant connectivity through simple chat commands
- **Community Managers** — manage live rooms and moderate participants without manual dashboard navigation
- **Product Teams** — quickly create and audit video rooms for new features or temporary events
- **Support Teams** — lookup session details and participant history to provide rapid technical assistance


## Available Tools
- **list_rooms**: Use this to find a room ID.

List all video rooms in the 100ms account
- **get_room**: Requires the unique room ID.

Get the configuration and details of a specific video room
- **create_room**: Use this when the user asks to start or host a new meeting space.

Create a new video room
- **update_room**: Requires the room ID.

Update the settings of an existing video room
- **list_sessions**: You can filter by room ID or status. Use this to find who attended past meetings.

List active or past video sessions
- **get_session**: Get the details and metadata of a specific video session
- **list_peers**: Requires the session ID.

List all participants currently inside an active video session
- **remove_peer**: Requires the session ID and the peer ID.

Kick or remove a specific participant from an active video session
- **list_recordings**: Can optionally filter by room ID or the status of the recording.

List cloud recordings of video rooms
