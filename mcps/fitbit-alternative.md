## Description
Connect your **Fitbit** account to any AI agent and gain deep insights into your physical health and wellness through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Active Zone Minutes (AZM)** — Retrieve time series data for specific dates or intervals to understand your heart-pumping activity.
- **Activity Summaries** — Get daily summaries of steps, calories, and distance, or list detailed activity logs.
- **Manual Logging** — Create or delete activity log entries and set daily or weekly activity goals directly through the agent.
- **Health Metrics** — Monitor blood glucose levels over specific intervals and track body goals like weight and body fat percentage.
- **Detailed Telemetry** — Access TCX data for specific activities to get granular performance metrics.

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your Fitbit Personal Access Token
3. Start analyzing your health trends from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client

### Who is this for?

- **Fitness Enthusiasts** — track progress against goals and analyze workout intensity without digging through mobile app menus.
- **Health-Conscious Users** — monitor vital metrics like blood glucose and weight trends alongside daily activity.
- **Data Analysts** — export and inspect raw activity telemetry (TCX) for deeper personal health research.
