Optimize content via Clearscope — create SEO reports, grade drafts using NLP, perform keyword research, and audit SERP competitors directly from any AI agent.

## Description
Connect your **Clearscope** account to any AI agent and take full control of your search discoverability through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **SEO Reports** — Create and list content reports to audit your topical authority and search performance
- **Content Grading** — Grade your drafts in real-time using advanced NLP to reach A++ status and outrank competitors
- **Keyword Research** — Identify traffic limits, CPC, and competitiveness for any keyword in the Clearscope database
- **SERP Analysis** — Extract competitor outlines, analyze term frequencies, and audit the top 30 Google results
- **Content Briefs** — Generate structural outlines and briefs with target word counts and required semantic topics
- **Workspace Management** — Navigate between different organizational workspaces to isolate your content projects

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your Clearscope API Key (Requires a Business or Enterprise plan)
3. Start optimizing your content from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client

### Who is this for?

- **Content Marketers** — grade drafts and refine SEO strategy without jumping between browser tabs
- **SEO Specialists** — perform deep keyword research and competitor gap analysis directly from your chat
- **Editors & Writers** — generate content outlines and briefs based on real-time SERP data
- **Product Teams** — monitor topical authority and discoverability for product documentation and landing pages


## Available Tools
- **list_reports**: Identify bounded logical arrays managing top-level Clearscope content SEO reports
- **get_report_details**: Perform structural extraction of properties driving active NLP scoring boundaries
- **create_report**: Provision a highly-available async parsing boundary running SERP execution hooks
- **grade_content**: Retrieve the exact structural matching verifying text against mapped NLP bounds
- **get_keyword_research**: Identify explicit Cloud calculations tracking theoretical Google traffic limits
- **list_terms**: Extracts explicitly attached NLP bounding variables organizing term frequencies
- **list_competitors**: Identify precise active arrays spanning native SERP competitor links
- **get_outline**: Extracts structural string arrays resolving AI-nested content hierarchies limit
- **get_brief**: Identify explicit instructions resolving writing boundaries
- **list_workspaces**: Retrieve the root identity mappings bounding specific organizational containers
