Manage B2B subscriptions, accounts, and financial metrics on Younium — the leading subscription management and billing platform.

**Category:** money-moves

## Description
Connect your **Younium** account to any AI agent and manage your B2B subscription infrastructure through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Subscription Lifecycle** — List all recurring billing subscriptions, retrieve historical versions, and create new subscription records directly from your agent
- **Account Management** — Browse your enterprise SaaS customer base, retrieve deep details including tax IDs, and provision new billing accounts
- **Financial Metrics** — Retrieve real-time MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), ARR, and TCV metrics for specific customer contracts
- **Invoice Auditing** — List and monitor all generated invoices and track their payment statuses (DRAFT, POSTED, PAID) across your account
- **Product Discovery** — Browse your billable product catalog and associated charge plans to understand your pricing inventory
- **Churn Control** — Immediately terminate specific subscription versions and manage contract effective dates through simple chat commands
- **Deep Discovery** — Quickly find unique account, subscription, and version IDs required for automated billing workflows

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your Younium JWT Token
3. Start managing your B2B subscriptions through Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client

No more manual navigation through complex ERP dashboards to check a subscription status. Your AI agent becomes your billing operations coordinator.

### Who is this for?

- **Finance Operations** — monitor MRR/ARR metrics and reconcile daily invoices through simple chat commands
- **Sales & Success Teams** — quickly verify customer subscription statuses and contract versions before renewal meetings
- **Accounting Teams** — audit account details and track payment statuses across the entire customer base
- **Product Managers** — browse the billable product catalog and verify charge plan configurations without manual navigation


## Available Tools
- **cancel_subscription**: Provide the subscription and version IDs plus the effective date.

Immediately terminates a specific subscription version
- **create_account**: Provide the account configuration as a JSON object.

Creates a new customer billing account record
- **create_subscription**: Provide subscription details as a JSON object.

Creates a new subscription for an existing account
- **get_account**: Retrieves comprehensive details for a specific Younium account
- **get_metrics**: Retrieves MRR, ARR, and TCV metrics for a specific subscription
- **get_subscription**: Retrieves details for a specific subscription, including historical versions
- **list_accounts**: Lists all customer accounts registered in the Younium ERP
- **list_invoices**: Lists all invoices generated by Younium
- **list_products**: Lists all products available in the Younium product catalog
- **list_subscriptions**: Lists all recurring billing subscriptions in the account
