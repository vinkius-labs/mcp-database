## Description
Empower your AI agent to orchestrate your entire search engine research workflow with **SerpApi**, the high-performance scraper for global search data. By connecting SerpApi to your agent, you transform complex multi-engine querying into a natural conversation. Your agent can instantly audit organic Google results, track Bing trends, and retrieve YouTube video metadata without you ever touching a browser. Whether you are conducting SEO audits or monitoring global search patterns, your agent acts as a real-time data aggregator, ensuring your intelligence is always grounded in diverse search engine data.

### What you can do

- **Multi-engine Auditing** — Query organic results from Google, Bing, Baidu, and Yahoo to maintain a clear view of global search visibility.
- **Visual Intelligence** — Search for videos on YouTube and retrieve detailed metadata, including titles and channel info.
- **Commerce Oversight** — Query Google Shopping results to maintain strict control over product pricing and availability across the web.
- **Search Intelligence** — Retrieve detailed snippets and direct links for any topic to assist in rapid information synthesis.
- **Historical Monitoring** — List search results across different engines to track brand mentions and content ranking over time.

## Available Tools
- **get_google_shopping**: Search Google Shopping results
- **search_baidu**: Search Baidu results
- **search_bing**: Search Bing organic results
- **search_google**: Search Google organic results
- **search_yahoo**: Search Yahoo results
- **search_youtube**: Search YouTube videos
