## Description
Connect your **TextYess** account to any AI agent and simplify how you manage your AI-driven sales, WhatsApp marketing campaigns, and conversational commerce analytics through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Campaign Management** — List all WhatsApp marketing campaigns and retrieve detailed analytics including opens, clicks, and revenue.
- **Order Tracking** — Monitor conversational commerce sales by listing all orders processed through AI agents or campaigns.
- **Product Sync Oversight** — List and inspect products synced from your e-commerce platform (Shopify, etc.) to your TextYess catalog.
- **Agent Interaction Monitoring** — List and review recent customer conversations with your AI shopping agent to ensure quality.
- **Integration Control** — List connected e-commerce platforms and verify active webhooks for product and order sync.
- **Organizational Insights** — Retrieve metadata for your organization and user profile directly from the agent.

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your TextYess API Key (found in your account dashboard)
3. Start managing your conversational commerce from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP client

### Who is this for?

- **E-commerce Managers** — quickly check campaign revenue and monitor product stock levels via simple AI commands.
- **Marketing Teams** — analyze WhatsApp broadcast performance and verify conversion rates directly from the workspace.
- **Business Owners** — get instant bird's-eye views of conversational sales and AI agent interactions via the AI assistant.


## Available Tools
- **get_campaign_conversions**: List orders generated by a campaign
- **get_campaigns_delivery_status**: Check campaigns deliverability
- **get_campaign_analytics**: Get analytics for a specific campaign
- **get_current_user**: Get authenticated user profile
- **get_organization_details**: Get organization information
- **get_product_details**: Get details for a specific product
- **list_marketing_campaigns**: List WhatsApp marketing campaigns
- **list_ai_conversations**: List AI agent interactions
- **list_connected_integrations**: List connected e-commerce platforms
- **list_all_orders**: List all conversational commerce orders
- **list_products**: List all synced products
- **list_configured_webhooks**: List active webhooks


## 💬 Prompt Examples

Here are some examples of how you can interact with the **TextYess** MCP server using an AI Agent (Claude, ChatGPT, etc.).

**👤 You:**
> "List all active marketing campaigns in my TextYess account."

**🤖 AI Agent:**
> I've retrieved your campaigns. You have 3 active broadcasts including 'Black Friday Preview', 'Abandoned Cart Recovery', and 'New Product Launch'. Which one would you like performance metrics for?

---

**👤 You:**
> "Show me the revenue generated by the 'Abandoned Cart' campaign (ID: camp_10293)."

**🤖 AI Agent:**
> Fetching analytics... The 'Abandoned Cart' campaign has generated $2,500 in revenue from 15 orders. It has a conversion rate of 12%. Shall I list the individual orders attributed to this campaign?

---

**👤 You:**
> "List the last 3 customer conversations with the AI shopping agent."

**🤖 AI Agent:**
> I've fetched the interaction logs. Here are the 3 most recent sessions: User_8823 (Completed Purchase), User_8824 (Question about sizing), and User_8825 (Inquiry about shipping). Would you like to see the full transcript for any of them?
