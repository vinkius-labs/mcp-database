## Description
Connect your **Craft.do** account to any AI agent and take full control of your premium writing experience and collaborative workspace through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **Document Navigation** — List and search all documents within your folders to identify specific workspace boundaries and content classifications
- **Block-Level Operations** — Retrieve detailed Block trees and append new structural elements (headings, lists, code, checklists) to any existing Page
- **Agile Document Creation** — Provision new highly-available Documents with specific Markdown content and titles natively from your chat
- **Daily Note Management** — Fetch temporal records and intricate networking mappings from your Calendar to audit daily journals and tasks
- **Task & Checklist Tracking** — Extract comprehensive mapped checklists across your workspace to monitor active progress and pending items
- **Content Mutation** — Safely substitute explicit Document values by patching Block trees and overwriting content limits through the REST API

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your Craft API Token (found in the 'Imagine' tab or Settings > API within the Craft app)
3. Start managing your notes and documents from Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible client

### Who is this for?

- **Writers & Researchers** — organize notes and append new research blocks without leaving the creative flow
- **Project Managers** — track tasks across multiple documents and audit daily progress journals using natural language
- **Product Teams** — quickly prototype document structures and manage collaboration blocks directly from the IDE
- **Power Users** — integrate their personal knowledge base and daily journals into automated AI workflows
