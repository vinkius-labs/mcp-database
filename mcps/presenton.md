# Presenton MCP Server

Automate presentation generation via Presenton — create AI slide decks and manage exports directly from any AI agent.

## Overview
**Category:** productivity
**Tools Count:** 12

## Description
Connect your **Presenton** account to any AI agent and simplify your presentation workflows through natural conversation.

### What you can do

- **AI Deck Generation** — Create complete presentation decks from text prompts, adjusting tone and slide count
- **Export Management** — Retrieve download links for your presentations in PPTX or PDF formats
- **Template Catalog** — List available AI-optimized templates to find the best look for your slides
- **History Tracking** — Monitor and manage your past presentations directly from your agent

### How it works

1. Subscribe to this server
2. Enter your Presenton API Key from your account settings
3. Start creating presentations using natural language prompts


## Available Tools
- **check_presenton_status**: Verify connectivity
- **delete_presentation**: Delete a presentation

- **list_presentations**: List presentations
- **list_templates**: List templates

- **update_presentation**: Update a presentation
