## Available Tools
- **get_cpi_inflation**: S. Consumer Price Index baseline. Identifies macroeconomic price shifts and consumer buying power anomalies.

Get Consumer Price Index (CPI-U) data for inflation tracking
- **get_jolts_data**: Critical for determining the Great Resignation impacts.

Get Job Openings (JOLTS) national metrics
- **get_nonfarm_payrolls**: Key metric for forecasting Federal Reserve interest rate movements.

Get Total Nonfarm Payroll employment (CES0000000001)
- **get_unemployment_rate**: S. unemployment figure generated by the Current Population Survey. Crucial for understanding labor force tightness and workforce health.

Get the National Unemployment Rate (LNS14000000) from the CPS
- **query_bls**: Use this instead of specific endpoints if you intimately know the underlying numerical code. Up to 50 concurrent lookbacks allowed.

Generic BLS v2 api timeseries query. Requires explicit BLS Series IDs
